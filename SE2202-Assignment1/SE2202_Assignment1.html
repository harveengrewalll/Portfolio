My Portfolio

A Sensational Solution to Rock Climbing for the Visually Impaired

I worked alongside a wonderful team to tackle real-world issues with creatively engineered solutions. My team and I created a reusable, portable, and wearable arm band that visually impaired climbers could use to feel their way up a rock climbing wall. This design featured miniature rock climbing pegs, magnets, and an arm band that allowed for flexibility.

Machine Learning and AI Meets the World of Finance

Recently, I have had the opportunity to work with a group of friends to explore the world of AI and machine learning through a hackathon. We worked to create a machine learning model that aimed to predict whether a transaction was fraudulent or not. This was done through Python, and we used numerous libraries to train the model through test cases.
